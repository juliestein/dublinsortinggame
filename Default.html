Sorting tips:

  • COMPOST accepts food scraps, food-soiled paper, and plant waste.
  • RECYCLE accepts clean paper and cardboard, glass bottles and jars, metal cans and clean aluminum foil, and rigid plastics #1-7 (bottles, jugs, and tubs).
  • LANDFILL is the destination for most other garbage, including plastic bags and wrappers as well as plastic-lined paper and plastic to-go containers. If in doubt, find out! Learn more about proper sorting by checking out Dublin's sorting guidelines!
  • SPECIAL HANDLING is required for items that are too big or hazardous for regular collection and processing. This includes bulky items, electronics, fuel canisters, and more. Learn more about what to do with these items here!
